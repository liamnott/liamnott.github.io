Heist

The Scoop

In this game, your mouse cursor is a thief. Move your mouse around the hazards and click on the green treasure in each level to progress. If you run into a hazard you will fail and you will never be able to pay back your student loans.

NOTE: This game is unfinished. Additionally, LiamNott.Com does not condone the act of theft of any kind.
